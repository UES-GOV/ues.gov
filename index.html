The Roots of the UES

The United Eastern States (UES) emerged during a period of profound upheaval, officially founded in 1997. By 2007, the UES formally ceded from the United States, igniting the Civil War. Over the years, the UES has established unique governance systems, defined by its dual leadership model and unyielding pursuit of self-determination. Despite ongoing conflict, the UES continues to grow, with its history shrouded in both intrigue and resilience.
